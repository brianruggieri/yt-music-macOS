--------------------------------------------------------------
AudioContext
  -> AudioWorkletNode (sine-sweep, 2ch out)
       |- viz.connectAudio(workletNode)   -- drives Butterchurn
       |- AnalyserNode                    -- for level readout in HUD
       |- GainNode(gain=0) -> destination -- keeps graph pulled (required)

Blob URL note
-------------
The AudioWorkletProcessor is loaded via a Blob URL in feed-spike.js.
If YT Music's CSP blocks blob: in worker-src, ctx.audioWorklet.addModule()
will throw and the HUD will show the error. Task 5 investigates the CSP fallback.
